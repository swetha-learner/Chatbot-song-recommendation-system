Frequently Asked Question

You May Ask

How does this website works?
At Chat with Boo page, you may asked to chat. The virtual assistant will communicate with you. The Trained model will analyse and predict your mood based on your input data. After analysing your emotions, based on the result, the model will recommend you the songs
Is loging in to the app important?
Absolutely NOT! We have developed this to assist the people whenever they want. So, Log In proocess is not required to have a conversation with Boo!
Can i use any kind of words?
We would recommend you to use formal words and friendly words as your harsh words will hurt Boo though he is your Virtual Companion! #spreadkindness
Is Chat with Boo free of charge?
Yes certainly! Our site won't asks for any subscription and charges.
What should i do after getting song recommendations?
You can listen to the songs that are suggested to you by directly clicking the link. The link will navigate you to Last.fm page where the songs are readily available to you to listen.
Can I chat using my mobile?
Yes! it is a responsive website with which you can diagnose using mobile phones , laptops , tablets.
How accuretly your website predict results?
we're not sure about 100% prediction because a Natural Language Processing model cannot be 100% accurate all the time.
Does your model chat with the person who is sad and alone all the time?
Yes! It is basically developed for the people who find difficulty while speaking with others and for those who are always sad and alone.
How can we know the upcoming updates of your site?
You get updated via social media. our social media links are provided in footer section of home page.
how far your site helpful to people?
This site will be helpfull for those who are really depressed and having worst feel about the world. We hope that our website will reduce their anxiety disorders and help them finding real friend as possible
